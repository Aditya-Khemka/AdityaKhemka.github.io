Answering Bell

Door Bell Using IR

T

he importance of door bell in today’s world? It’s pretty important. The answer to all the guests that knock at your door is initiated by your door bell. “But what if you cannot answer to your guest immediately?”

Where did the Idea come from?

The above question was aroused by the respected Chairperson of our school Mrs. Bhanumati Neelkanthan. The 90 year old lady requested for such a device that would answer her guests from a distance.

We friends decided to make a device with whose help you could answer your guests from a distance avoiding the  use to shout from the balcony or rushing up and down in your house.

After much discussion, we came up with the following decisions:

We defined the problem as:”The people in old age and in certain situations people come across such problems in which they are unable to answer their guests”

  1. A speaker that would start as soon as the door bell is pressed
  2. A door bell made using AI
  3. An app to communicate with the guests
  4. A display to inform the guests

        

How did it develop?

The simple door bells available in the market are a simple reactive approach. The simple answering bells are interactive until someone receives the invite and to do this one has to go to the receive the interactive call.

But, this device is not like the above. One can answer the guest even from a distance.

How does it work?

       This gadget satisfies its name as it can answer to all those guests who knock at the door-step. It does not have any Artificial Inteligence but can answer to your guests according to your will.

This thing is made using the famous micro-controller Arduino®. It is also provided with an L.C.D. screen where it can display the message. A remote is used to command the computer. According to your command, it will display the message on the L.C.D.

Real life example

Just suppose that you are busy with an important work and just then a postman arrives. Instead of running to the balcony and asking the guy to stop, you can always do the same just by pressing a button.
