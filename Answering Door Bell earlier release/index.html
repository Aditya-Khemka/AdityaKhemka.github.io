Answering bell

This gadget satisfies its name as it can answer to all those guests who knock at the door-step. It does not have any Artificial Inteligence but can answer to your guests according to your will.

For ex: You are busy with your work and it is just about to complete but at the same moment, a post-man arrives. Here, you can ask him to wait for a while just by pressing a button.

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

How does it work?

This thing is made using the famous micro-controller Arduino®. It is also provided with an L.C.D. screen where it can display the message. A remote is used to command the computer. According to your command, it will display the message on the L.C.D., reducing your effort to climb down the stairs or shout from the balcony for a wait...
