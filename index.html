About the vehicle:

The vehicle justifies its name as it can carry out multiple tasks at a time.

It reduces the work of six farm vehicles and two machines to its own self and so uses less fuel, thus reducing the areas carbon foot-print. It looks a-symmetrical from externally. It is also provided with attracting and hi-tech, features discussed later about.

Components and Equipments:

There are a whole lot of external components and equipments some of which are detachable and some not. These components are like the heart of the vehicle. The vehicle has been provided with the following equipments.

  • The shower
  • The seed dispersal unit
  • The plough
  • The Route cleaner
  • The carriage
  • The WeedRoller
  • LED lights
  • Cameras
  • Main wheels
  • Supporting wheels
  • Shower and plough supporting wheels
  • Water/ pesticide/ fertilizer storage unit
  • Weather station
  • Operator cabin
  • Ultrasonic wave antenna
  • Suspension System

The vehicle, in a view

(Block diagram of the non detachable parts).

It contains all the non detachable parts and joints for the detachable part. The cabin and the liquid storage covers the maximum area. The vehicle has to be powered by an engine producing 700 – 800 H.P. in order to drive this massive machine.

The Shower

The Shower serves as a sprinkler for any liquid inside its respective storage unit, may it be water, pesticides or fertilizer.

This type of irrigation ensures that the plant gets the right amount of the needed chemical .The farmer can adjust the jet as per the need.  Also this conserves water, as it follows drop irrigation system. This also ensures that the whole field is not covered with water that seldom damages the crops.

It is provided with two supporting wheels on either side, each with a width of 15 cm and diameter of 2 feet, thus keeping the shower 24 inches above the surface. The supporting wheels are free to rotate on their own axis upto 45°. It is connected with the frame of the shower and has a superior suspension system.

The Seed dispersal unit

The seed dispersal unit is designed to hold lots of seed and drop the desired amount of seed on the way. It is just in line with the plough and so there is no chance for the seed to be wasted. The farmer can change the quantity of seed to be dropped per second as the need arises.

The seed dispersing unit is placed on one of the joints on either side of the vehicle

This too is supported by supporting wheels on either side, each having a radius of 1.5 feet and width of 6 cm.. The seed dispersal’s frame is attached to the chassis of the vehicle. The supporting wheel is also provided with superior suspension. The supporting wheels can freely rotate upto 45° on either side. The opening of the dispersal unit opens at regular intervals and drops the entire amount of seed in the ‘Independent size opening’.  

The Plough

The plough here is similar to the various ploughs that are available now-a-days in the market easily. It loosens the soil for the seeds to be sowed and also removes any weeds incoming on the way.

The block  diagram demonstrates a reversible plough combined with a traditional plough to loosen the soil and further can be supported by ploughing wheels. The plough’s height is 2.50 feet.

The plough too is supported by supporting wheels placed on either sides of the frame of the plough. The plough’s supporting wheels have a width of 10 cm and the radius can be varied among the following:

  • 1.25 feet
  • 1.50 feet

The Route cleaner

The route cleaner justifies its name. It can be installed at the space provided just in front of the cabin.

This device is inclined and provided with a conveyer belt with a storage for the bulky boulders at the back so that they do not these do not posses a problem afterward.

The wheels for the storage are 1 feet in radius and 6 cm in width. There is a continuous belt for the incline thus providing the needed torque to lift the boulders. The sieve allows particles less than 50mm in radius to pass through.

A continuous track (left) and a graphical illustration of the route cleaner (right)

The Cabin

The cabin is the brain of the vehicle. It is raised 4 feet above the wheel’s reach, so as to make it easier for the farmer to have a clear view of everything in front.

It has Solar panels on the top which power the weather station which is installed just above this cockpit. The solar panels also store some energy  so that the weather station can function at night times and also power the front and back blinking LED’s (these LED’s are used to warn any one around about the movement of the vehicle, just as the on-ground airport vehicles).

The cabin is also provided with a screen for the farmer to view the cameras and the weather data and also to navigate through the GPS, if at all needed.  

The weed remover

Weeds are really a huge problem in modern agriculture and there are a number of machines available to de-root it from the land, now-a-days. This vehicle uses WeedRoller to remove the weeds. This does not really remove it but de-roots it as the weeds usually are a problem because they suck the nutrients from the soil.

It does not need any additional supporting wheels. Their radius is 30’’.

Note: WeedRoller is a registered weed removing device developed by Polet Inc.

The carriage

The carriage s a simple trolley that can be installed at the back, helping in crop harvesting. It does not use electricity and is provided with a flap, in case needed.

Storage

The storage stores the liquid to be sprayed by the shower. It can also be used for storing crops. It is made up of tick plastic and provided with a lid. It is rectangular in shape and has a depth of 2 feet .

Led Light

The vehicle is provided with various LED lights to help the farmer get a clear view of the surroundings even if is night.

Hook with power cables

These are the joints on the vehicle which not only do the work of combining the external component but also provide the required electricity to run the machine. It is provided at four points on the vehicle.

Hook without power supply

These hooks do the simple work of joining the external component to the machine. These are very strong and have suspension systems.

Weather-proof camera

The vehicle is also provided with weather-proofcameras helping the farmer to have a convenient view of the rear view and the side views.

 

The Emitting antenna

The antenna emits ultra-sonic waves in the surroundings to fight the insects and pests that may have crept in the field.

Rear Wheels

The rear wheels are no less than 5 feet in radius and a width of 20 cm, to balance the heavy weight of the vehicle. On he rear side, there are 8 wheels of the same size. There are four wheels on either sides, in pair.

Front wheels

The front wheels have a radius of 3.5 feet and a width of 20cm. There is one front wheel on either side.

 Weather station

The weather station collects weather data such as the humidity, temperature, wind direction etc. It is protected by a weather-proof box and is powered by the solar panels.

 Suspension System

The vehicle is provided with a superior spring – hydraulic suspension system. This is provided at various places. Besides the wheels, these also reside at the plough, the WeedRoller, the Route Cleaner and the carriage. Every supporting wheel is provided with suspension systems, big or small.
