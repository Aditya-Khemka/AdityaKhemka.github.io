The dual torch

The dual torch is the first of its kind. Accepting its name, it has something dual than a normal torch, it is the light. This torch is not provided with bulb(s) or LED(s) only at one side but two. There are two 12v operative LED(s) at the front, facing towards the front itself, and one 9v LED at the bottom facing downwards.

----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- -----

Advantages:

An extra LED below will enable the user to keep a watch on his steps. This will be extremely helpful if the person is ascending or descending stairs.

 ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- ----- -----

What’s new?

There are many new features of the torch that can be extremely helpful. Some of them are:

  1. An extra LED

This torch is provided with two LED’s. One at the front and the other downward.

  1. Extra switches

The torch is provided with two sets of switches. The first is for the LED facing the front. The three switches here are labelled as: ‘off’, ‘on’ and ‘bright glow’. The second set is for the LED facing downward. The two switches here are labelled as : ‘off’ and ‘on’.

  1. Radium strips

There are radium strips running across the body which will glow in the dark. These will help the user to find the torch, in case it gets dark.
